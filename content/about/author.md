## My Main Values

I like using [Shalom H. Schwartz's theory of the 10 basic human values](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theory_of_Basic_Human_Values). Having someone sort them from most to least important is a quick way to get a general idea of what they're about. Consequently, it also serves as a good standardized comparison tool. The middle ones are the least interesting. The top and bottom three are the most telling.

With that said, here are my results:

- Top Three: 1. Self-Direction, 2. Universalism, and 3. Stimulation.
- Bottom Three: 8. Achievement, 9. Power, and 10. Tradition.

Another way to word my two main values is Self-Determination & Fairness, or Universal Agency if we combine them into one.
